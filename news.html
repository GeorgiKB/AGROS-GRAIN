About This Publication

Agros-98 AD is a Bulgarian oilseed processor and exporter operating since 1998. We grow and process flaxseed, sunflower, coriander, pumpkin, and walnut at our integrated facility in Suvorovo, Varna Province. Our editorial content reflects real market conditions we observe as an active exporter — not generic industry commentary. Articles are updated as regulations or market conditions change.

We cover topics including EU food safety regulations (EFSA, Regulation 2023/915), crop forecasts and growing conditions in Bulgaria, seed quality parameters and grading standards, cold-chain logistics, and packaging options for wholesale buyers. If you have a specific question not covered by our articles, contact us directly — we respond to all genuine trade enquiries within one business day.

From field to final packaging — quality you can trace. Suvorovo & Varna, Bulgaria.
