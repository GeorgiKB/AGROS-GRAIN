Premium European Yellow Flaxseed (Golden Flaxseed)

Yellow flaxseed—widely known internationally as golden flaxseed—is a highly sought-after variety of Linum usitatissimum characterised by its distinctively pale seed coat. At Agros-98 AD, we are a leading bulk wholesale supplier of European golden flaxseed, cultivating and processing our products natively in the Varna region of Bulgaria under strict FSSC 22000 quality systems.

Why Choose Yellow Over Brown Flaxseed?

From a nutritional standpoint, yellow and brown varieties are nearly identical. Both boast an impressive 38–42% oil content and provide robust levels of ALA (Alpha-Linolenic Acid) omega-3 fatty acids and dietary fibre. However, wholesale bulk yellow flaxseed commands a premium due to its aesthetic appeal and its marginally milder, more neutral flavour profile.

Main Applications in the Food Industry

  • Premium Retail Packaging: The visually striking golden colour is highly preferred by consumers in health-food stores and the wellness sector.
  • Bakery & Artisan Bread Inclusion: The lighter seed coat allows it to blend seamlessly into white and artisan breads without darkening the crumb visually.
  • Cold-Pressed Oil Extraction: Just like the brown variant, golden seeds produce a beautifully rich and highly sought-after functional oil.
  • Functional Foods & Cereals: Ideal for inclusion in muesli mixes, granola bars, and plant-based protein formulations.

Bulk Supply & Wholesale Packaging

As a direct producer rather than a trading intermediary, we offer high-volume consistency. Our golden flaxseed is heavily machine-cleaned to reach an exceptional minimum purity of 99.95%. We accommodate industrial buyers by packaging in 1,000 kg big bags for mass-manufacturing lines, as well as 25 kg food-grade paper bags ready for swift distribution to retail repackaging facilities.
