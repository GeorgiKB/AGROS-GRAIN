Описание на продукта

Our cold-pressed linoleic sunflower oil is pressed from non-GMO Bulgarian sunflower seeds at temperatures below 40°C, preserving the natural tocopherol content (vitamin E), delicate flavour, and full spectrum of fatty acids that are partially destroyed by conventional solvent extraction and high-heat refining.

Linoleic sunflower oil — the conventional type — has a fatty acid profile dominated by linoleic acid (omega-6), typically 55–70%. It has a light, clean sunflower flavour, pale golden colour, and broad application across the food industry: salad dressings, dips, cold sauces, premium cooking oils, cosmetic formulations, and nutraceutical bases.

We press to order and bottle in dark glass or food-grade tin to protect against oxidation. Peroxide value and free fatty acid content are tested per batch. Bulk drum supply is available for food manufacturers and cosmetic producers.
