Step 02 3× SORTEX optical sorting cascade

Step 02 — Precision Cleaning

99.98% purity.
No compromise.

Our facility runs three SORTEX optical sorters operating in cascade — each pass removing what the previous missed. The result is product that consistently exceeds food-grade specifications at global standard.

3× SORTEX Optical Sorting Three-pass cascade sorting eliminates discoloured seeds, foreign matter and off-spec material to 99.98% purity.
Multi-Stage Pre-Cleaning Scalping, gravity separation and fine-mesh sieving before optical sorting ensure consistent incoming quality.
In-Line Metal Detection Final metal detection on every filled bag. Zero tolerance. Every lot documented and traceable.
🏅 FSSC 22000 & ISO 22000 certified — audited annually
