Who We Are

Rooted in Bulgarian Agriculture

Agros-98 AD is a Bulgarian agricultural company producing and exporting seeds, cold-pressed oils, and specialty flours to food manufacturers across Europe and beyond.

Founded in 1994, we control the complete supply chain — from cultivating low-pesticide crops on our own farmland, through mechanical cold-press extraction, to final packaging and export documentation.

Our production facility in Suvorovo processes sunflower, coriander, flaxseed, pumpkin, and walnut crops under strict food safety protocols. At the moment we are the largest European producer of bulk yellow flaxseed (golden flaxseed). Every batch is traceable from the field to the label.

Own Farmland

We cultivate sunflower, coriander, flaxseed, and other crops on land we own in the Varna region — no third-party sourcing, full traceability from seed to shipment.

🌿

Low-Pesticide

Our cultivation program uses minimal pesticide inputs and follows integrated pest management practices, producing cleaner raw material for food-grade applications.

⚙️

Full Integration

From cleaning and cold-press extraction to packaging and labelling, every step of the value chain happens in our own facility in Suvorovo — no outsourced processing.

Food Safety Docs

We supply full documentation: COA, Certificate of Origin, phytosanitary certificates, and EUR.1 preferential origin documents for every shipment to EU buyers.
