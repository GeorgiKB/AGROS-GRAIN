Product Description

Agros-98's Bakery Grade hulled sunflower kernels are produced from sunflower varieties grown on our own farmland in the Varna region under a low-pesticide cultivation program. After harvest, seeds go through multiple cleaning and gravity-separation stages to achieve purity of 99.9% or above.

The bakery size range — 550 to 600 pieces per ounce — delivers consistent kernel size that toasts evenly and integrates uniformly into bread doughs, rolls, crackers, and granola. The white-to-light-grey colour provides excellent visual appeal in finished products.

Processing takes place in our integrated Suvorovo facility using equipment calibrated for food-grade production. Full documentation including COA, Certificate of Origin, and phytosanitary certificates is supplied with every shipment.

We supply raw hulled kernels only. Roasting is not part of our current process. Many of our bakery customers roast in-line or via partner roasters.
